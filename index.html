Our Story

More Than a Café

Point Seven Coffee is a cultural experience rooted in the warmth of Emirati hospitality and elevated by global culinary artistry. Founded in the UAE, we've quickly become a lifestyle destination, known for our signature dishes like the saffron-infused Aseeda Fondant and artisanal coffee blends.

With flagship locations in Fujairah and Sharjah, we're expanding into key regional markets including Riyadh, Doha, Abu Dhabi, and Muscat, bringing our unique blend of flavors, storytelling, and community connection to a wider audience.
